`inspect_response=True` now ENFORCES phase-3/4 disruptions on all three adapters (Flask, Starlette/FastAPI, Django). Earlier releases ran the response-side rules but let the upstream response through on a disruption — the WAF was effectively monitor-only on phase 3/4. The ASGI adapter buffers `http.response.start` and the response body so the block can replace the headers; SSE / chunked-download responses are fully buffered when inspection is enabled. `docs/threat-model.md` is updated.
