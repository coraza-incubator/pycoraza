Add `body_limits=BodyLimits(...)` to every adapter (Flask, Starlette / FastAPI, Django) to bound WAF body buffering. Bodies up to `max_in_memory` (default 1 MB) stay in RAM; the overflow up to `max_total` (default 32 MB) is spooled to a `tempfile.SpooledTemporaryFile` and fed to Coraza in chunks. Past `max_total`, the new `on_overflow` knob — `"block"` (default, fail-closed → 413), `"skip"`, or `"evaluate_partial"` — picks the trade-off. Defaults make a 1 k RPS / 1 MB upload workload allocate ~1 MB per request instead of ~2.
